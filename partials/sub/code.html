Newsico Code of Ethics

1. Prioritize honesty in reporting and ensure that accurate information is delivered to the public.

2. Seek the truth and report it with accuracy and fairness.

3. Show respect for all individuals and groups, regardless of their backgrounds or beliefs, and do not discriminate based on any characteristic.

4. Provide balanced coverage and representation of all sides of an issue, striving for fairness in reporting.

5. Seek out and reflect the perspectives and experiences of a wide range of communities, promoting diversity in newsrooms and coverage.

6. Maintain independence and avoid conflicts of interest, striving to be unbiased in reporting.

7. Acknowledge and correct mistakes, ensuring that accurate information is provided to the public.

8. Be transparent in reporting, providing clear and accurate information about sources, methods, and motives.

9. Avoid speculation and stick to the facts, upholding accuracy and truthfulness in reporting.

10. Support and defend freedom of the press, upholding the right to report the news without fear of censorship or repression.
